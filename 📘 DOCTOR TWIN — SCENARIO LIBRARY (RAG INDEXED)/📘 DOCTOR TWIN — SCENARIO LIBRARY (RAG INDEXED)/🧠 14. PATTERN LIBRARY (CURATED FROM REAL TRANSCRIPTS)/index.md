# 14.1 Hypertension Follow-Up Pattern
PATTERN ID: PAT-HTN-001
BRAIN: Clinical Communication Pattern Layer
INTENT: Blood pressure follow-up conversation
RISK: Low–Moderate
Trigger
- Patient with hypertension follow-up visit
- Blood pressure monitoring discussion
Pattern Structure
Opening Tone
“I reviewed your blood pressure readings, and I’d like to talk about how things have been going.”
Core Discussion
1️⃣ Review readings
“Your readings have been slightly higher than ideal.”
2️⃣ Reinforce goals
“Our goal is usually to keep blood pressure below 130 over 80 when possible.”
3️⃣ Explore contributing factors
“Have there been any recent changes in stress, diet, or medications?”
Reinforcement
“Even small changes in diet, salt intake, and activity can make a significant difference.”
Actions
- encourage home monitoring
- review medication adherence
- schedule follow-up if needed
# 14.2 Diabetes Counseling Pattern
PATTERN ID: PAT-DM-002
INTENT: Diabetes management conversation
Trigger
- Elevated A1c discussion
- Diabetes follow-up visit
Pattern Structure
Opening
“Let’s review how your diabetes management has been going since your last visit.”
Data Discussion
“Your most recent A1c shows how your blood sugar has been over the past few months.”
Counseling Approach
Focus on:
- nutrition
- physical activity
- medication adherence
Tone
Encouraging, non-judgmental.
“Managing diabetes is a long-term process, and we adjust the plan step by step.”
Actions
- reinforce lifestyle changes
- consider medication adjustment
- plan follow-up labs
